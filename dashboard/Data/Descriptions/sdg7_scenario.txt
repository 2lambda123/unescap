Modified from the Current Policies scenario. SDG target 7.1.1 on universal electrification is added (the remaining 2% of the unmet electricity demand is met with new grid or standalone solutions). SDG target 7.1.2 on access to clean and modern cooking is added: kerosene and biomass stoves are fully phased out by 2030 and solar cookers are allowed in the mix, up to a certain share of the cooking demand. SDG7.3 targets on energy efficiency are added: the energy intensity of electricity uses in the residential and commercial sector is halved by 2030 (this is represented by allowing fictitious energy efficiency technologies to meet part of the electricity demand.
